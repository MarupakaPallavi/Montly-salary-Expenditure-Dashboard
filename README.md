# Montly-salary-Expenditure-Dashboard
Simple and effective monthly salary expenditure tracker and savings dashboard.

**Project Overview**:

This Monthly Salary Expenditure Dashboard is a streamlined financial management tool designed to help individuals track their income, monitor spending habits, and optimize savings. Built with a focus on the Indian financial context (INR), it provides a bird's-eye view of your financial health in one single sheet.

**Key Features:**

Income vs. Expense Tracking: Easily visualize your cash flow and identify where your money goes each month.

Categorized Spending: Break down costs into Fixed Expenses, Variable Spending, and Debt Repayments.

Savings Goal Monitoring: Dedicated sections to track progress toward long-term savings and emergency funds.

Automated Calculations: Built-in logic to provide instant totals, percentages, and remaining balances.

**Note :**
"Simply download the file, input your monthly figures in the designated cells, and the dashboard will update your financial summary automatically."
